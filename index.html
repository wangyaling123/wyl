The Wei, Jin and Six Dynasties was a special period of the fusion of national thoughts. In the process of the fusion of history and culture, a lot of literature was left. In this paper, the stone inscriptions of the Han and Wei Dynasties and the Six Dynasties are sorted and classified, and the data visualization analysis is carried out in order to have a more comprehensive and specific understanding of the research on the stone inscriptions of the Wei and Jin Dynasties
